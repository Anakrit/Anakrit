Frequently asked questions

Is CuraLink free to use?

Yes. The MVP is free for both patients and researchers to explore and save resources that matter.

Where does the content come from?

We surface structured data from trusted sources including ClinicalTrials.gov, PubMed, and leading medical journals.

Can I invite my care team?

Absolutely. You can share direct links to recommended experts, trials, and forums right from the platform.
